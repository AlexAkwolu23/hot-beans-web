Philip Heinz

Founder & CEO

Philip Heinz, the founder and CEO of Hot Beans Web, has over 30 years of experience in technology and digital solutions. With a strong background in software development and business management, Philip founded the company in 1864 with a vision to provide cutting-edge digital solutions to businesses worldwide. Under his leadership, Hot Beans Web has grown from a small start-up to a leader in web development. He is passionate about innovation, and his goal is to continue driving the company towards new heights while keeping the values of quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction at the forefront of everything the company does.
